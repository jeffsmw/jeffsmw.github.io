Overview of the Event

Think Engineering is an annual networking event organized and hosted by the IEEE Student Branches at UBC, SFU, and BCIT in conjunction with the UBC Electrical and Computer Engineering Student Society. Our aim is to connect and engage students in Greater Vancouver with industry representatives.

Keynote Speech

An industry leader will give a speech on advice for success drawn from his or her own personal experience.

Networking

An open panel discussion with professionals with varying backgrounds and experience.

Cocktail Reception

A chance for students from UBC, SFU, & BCIT to meet and connect.

Get your tickets from the UBC IEEE office MCLD 319; ticket sales end Friday Jan 23rd.

Companies

Learn about each company attending Think Engineering 2015. The companies are here to get to know you, and for you to get to know them! This is a great opportunity to build professional relationships with managers and employers.

Alpha Technologies Ltd. – Power Solutions

Alpha Technologies Ltd., a member of The Alpha Group, is an established leader in the design, manufacture, service and installation of powering solutions for the Telecom, CATV (Canada), Traffic, Security, Medical, Industrial, and Renewable Energy industries.

BC Cancer Agency – Healthcare Solutions and Research

The BC Cancer Agency, an agency of the Provincial Health Services Authority, provides a province-wide, population-based cancer control program for the residents of British Columbia and the Yukon.

BC Hydro – Energy Systems

BC Hydro’s primary business activities are the generation and distribution of electricity. For years the Transmission of that electricity has been facilitated by the BC Transmission Corporation on behalf of BC Hydro and other power providers in B.C.

Broadcom –Hardware and Software Systems

Broadcom Corporation is a major technology innovator and global leader in semiconductors for wired and wireless communications. Broadcom products deliver voice, video, data and multimedia to and throughout the home, the office and mobile environment.

CH2M HILL – Engineering

As a global leader in engineering, procurement and construction management, CH2M HILL has the human and technical resources, the international footprint, and the depth of know-how and experience to help clients achieve success in any corner of the world.

Fransen Engineering – Consulting

Fransen Engineering is a dynamic, multi-discipline team of specialists providing consulting engineering services to support industrial clients in maintaining and upgrading their facilities. We pride ourselves in delivering a collaborative and flexible approach to projects.
