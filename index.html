About the event.

Think Engineering is an annual networking event organized and hosted by the IEEE Student Branches at UBC, SFU, and BCIT in conjunction with the UBC Electrical and Computer Engineering Student Society. Our aim is to connect and engage students in Greater Vancouver with industry representatives.

Keynote Speech

An industry leader will give a speech on advice for success drawn from his or her own personal experience.

Networking

An open panel discussion with professionals with varying backgrounds and experience.

Cocktail Reception

A chance for students from UBC, SFU, & BCIT to meet and connect.
